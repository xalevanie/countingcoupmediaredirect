COUNTING COUP MEDIA

Counting Coup Media is a group of ambitious filmmakers who are focused on bringing a new kind of quality and style to the Native community. With over ten years of experience in video, photography, digital signage, animation and sound design, they can handle any project that hits their table.

The company is currently working in association with the Kalispel Tribe, Camas Path, and the Spokane Tribe.

The short film is a dramedy that follows a young modern day Native named Joseph who works an office job. He's continually haunted by dreams of four Native riders on the plains from hundreds of years ago. They call to him, but he's not sure why.

Red Road was submitted to the LA Skins Festival. A huge Thank You to all who supported and helped the making of this film, including the Spokane Arts Grants Award -SAGA.
